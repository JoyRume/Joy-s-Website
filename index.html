Our Dogs

Rottweilers

the security guy

. Rottweilers have a strong reputation for protection work and guarding. Their size, loud bark, and protective nature have the potential to make them excellent protectors. Unfortunately, years of poor breeding has diluted previously strong and highly driven working lines. This has led to temperamentally unsound dogs we would not view as being appropriate for family protection work. However, on occasion, this is not the case, and we recently sourced a number of Rottweilers that meet our very specific requirements to train as family protection dogs.

Maltese

The Player

The Maltese is a small, elegant dog breed known for its long, silky white coat and affectionate, playful personality. Size and Weight: The Maltese is a toy breed, weighing under 7 pounds, with 4-6 pounds often being idea Activity Level: They are energetic but require only occasional exercise to keep them healthy and happy Grooming: Their long, silky coat needs to be brushed a few times a week, and they do not have an undercoat and shed very little. Temperament: Maltese dogs are affectionate, playful, intelligent, and trainable. They are good with children and make loving and loyal companion. Living Environment: They are well suited to apartment or condo living The Maltese is known for its aristocratic appearance and has been a favored companion of royalty over the years. They are generally good with older children but may be snappish with younger children, so early socialization is important.

The kitty one!

pug

The Pug is a small dog breed with a distinctive appearance, including a wrinkled face, short snout, and curled tail. Size and Weight: Pugs are a toy breed, weighing between 14-18 pounds and standing about 12 inches tall at the withers Activity Level: Pugs are energetic and playful but do not require a lot of exercise. They are well-suited for apartment living Grooming: Their short, fine coat requires minimal grooming, but their wrinkles need to be cleaned regularly to prevent skin infections Temperament: Pugs are known for their even-tempered, affectionate, and outgoing personalities. They are good with children and make excellent family pets Health: Pugs are prone to certain health issues, including breathing problems, eye problems, and skin infections due to their wrinkles Pugs are a popular breed due to their charming personalities and distinctive appearance. They are well-suited for apartment living and make excellent family pets. However, potential owners should be aware of their health issues and be prepared to provide regular care to keep them healthy and happy.
